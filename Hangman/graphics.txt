    Your role is to save hangman's life by guessing the name of capital.

    The letters of a password to guess are marked with #.
    Every # responds to the letter of capital.
    If there is space between "#" symbols, then the capital's name consists more than one word.

    You can try to guess a single letter or the whole name.

    At the beginning of the game, You have 0 points. 
    If You do not guess the letter you will gain 1 penalty point. 
    If You do not guess the whole password, You gain 2 penalty points. 

    Gaining 6 points or more is equivalent to Hangman's death.

    When You win, check if You have not broken the record!
