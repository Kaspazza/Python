  Welcome to hangman, his life depends on you!
  Choose Your option by pressing a number and Enter:
  
      1.Start game
      2.How to play
      3.Show leaderboard
      4.Exit

  At the beginning of the game You have 5 points. Try to guess the capital of the country.
  The letters of a password to guess are marked with #. You can try to guess a single letter
  or the whole name. If You do not guess the letter you will lose 1 point. If You do not
  guess the whole password, You lose 2 points. When You lose all points You lose. When You 
  win, check if You have not broken the record! Have fun!
